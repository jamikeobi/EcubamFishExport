Refund Policy

1. Refund Eligibility

Due to the nature of our products, we do not accept returns or offer refunds for perishable goods, including fish and seafood, unless the product is damaged or spoiled upon arrival.

If you receive a damaged or spoiled product, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. Provide us with your order number and photographic evidence of the damage or spoilage.

2. Refund Process

Once we receive your request and verify the issue, we will process your refund or send a replacement product at no additional cost.

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 3 business days.
